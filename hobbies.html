Hobbies

Jack's favorite sport has always been golf. The reason it's his favorite is because he enjoys being outside and enjoying the weather without getting too tired. He traveled across the country to play the sport while he was in the military.

Jack's parents - Jack Sr and Maria Saenz - were both raised Catholic, and taught their children to be devoted Christians themselves. Jack regularly goes to church on Sunday, and has frequently donated time, money, and clothes to various charities.

Jack was a fan of Elvis Presley's music ever since he first heard it in the 50s. Miraculously, he even met the King when Presley did miiltary service - during his training in Texas. Jack is still a fan of Presley's music today.
