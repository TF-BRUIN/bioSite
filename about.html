Biography

Jack Forey Jr was born in San Antonio, Texas, to Jack Forey Sr and Maria Saenz-Forey in December 1934. He was the youngest of ten siblings (seven brothers and two sisters), and spent his early youth and adolescence working either in the cotton fields of rural Texas or in his father’s auto shop. He went to school throughout the 1940s, but would drop out of high school and join the military. He would never see deployment on an active battlefield, but would travel across the US both for his basic training and to help train new recruits as he climbed the military ranks.

In his free time, Jack’s biggest hobby was golf. Whenever he could get away from work, he would travel around the United States and play golf in many different states and environments – from New York to New Mexico. He would play in some local unprofessional golf tournaments, and reportedly did very well. He was also a devout Catholic, like his parents, and frequently donated his time or income to various religious charities. Finally, he claims to have met Elvis Presley when the singer did his training in Texas, and was a fan of his music both at the time and in the present day.

Eventually, Jack would go to become the manager of his base’s commissary – a military grocery store that sells items to military service members and their families. It’s here where he proved himself to be a highly capable boss, and he would stay in this position for the rest of his military career. It’s here he would also meet secretary Viva Steffens, who he would later marry in 1973 and then have their only child, Jack Forey III. His family would eventually end up living in Bellevue, NE so Jack could work at Offutt Air Force Base (AFB). He would work there for almost 20 years before eventually retiring in 1996.

In the present day, Jack is almost 90 years old. He currently has two grandchildren, Jack Forey IV and Truman Forey (myself), and one great-grandchild through Jack IV, Nathan Forey. He remains in good health, and spends his time staying in shape so he can keep playing golf in his old age.
